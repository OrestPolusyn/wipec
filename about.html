About Us

A little bit more about Us

Started in 1990th by working on the floor now we are a team with almost 200 people from all over the world. We are researchers, data scientists, analysts, developers and traders. We are inspired and truly connected to create the most robust infrastructure in trading environment on global markets bringing and bridging the most sophisticated technology with reliable liquidity to our clients(зачерктнуто) partners.

We want our clients to become our partners thus we leverage mutual opportunities in order to have real competitive edge in such a high volatile, extremely risky and competitive trading environment. We offer a global perspective. We are service driven, transparent and engaged. We know all ins and outs, we have extensive contacts and sources in the brokerage and custodians that brings a unique global perspective and long-term value.

The foundation of our business is an inclusive environment which we value the most. We allow our partners to simplify and scale their business with ease and stability by using our services. We are passionate about what we build. It is essential for us to work together as a team achieving new goals constantly, that’s why we are focusing on people.
